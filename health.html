Heath and Technology

Warfarin Dosage Detector

Warfarin is life saving drug used by patients suffering from heart ailments as a blood thinner or anti-coagulant. It’s dosage must be accurate and it changes over time with each subsequent dose. Currently the doctors use a chart based system which is inaccurate and time consuming. Our ML based model processes a time series data of patients from the IWPC Dataset and helps predict the initial dosage and the forthcoming maintenance dosages of the drug for the individual patients based on various parameters.

Pneumonia Predictor

Pneumonia is an infection of the lungs with a range of possible causes. It can be a serious and life-threatening disease. Our model identifies Pneumonia through chest X-Ray using deep learning and image processing tools for better diagnose of the disease.
